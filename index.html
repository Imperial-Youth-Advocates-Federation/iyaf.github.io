Who We Are

Building the Future Through Youth Advocacy

The Imperial Youths Advocates Federation (IYAF) is a youth-led organization dedicated to empowering young people to become active participants in achieving the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.

We believe every young person deserves access to quality education, meaningful opportunities, and a platform to make their voice heard. Through structured programs, mentorship, and community engagement, IYAF transforms potential into impact.

Founded in 2024, IYAF has quickly grown into a dynamic movement of youth advocates who are passionate about building a better, more sustainable, and more equitable world for all.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

🎯

Our Mission

To empower young people with the knowledge, skills, and platforms they need to champion the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals and create lasting positive change in their communities and beyond.

🔭

Our Vision

A world where every young person is an informed, active, and confident advocate for sustainable development — contributing to a just, peaceful, and prosperous future for all people on Earth.

⭐

Our Values

We are guided by integrity, inclusivity, innovation, and impact. We celebrate diversity, champion youth voices, foster collaboration across borders, and hold ourselves to the highest standards of service.

What We Offer

Our Programs & Certifications

Join a transformative program and earn a recognized certificate upon completion. All programs are designed to equip youth with real-world skills and SDG knowledge.

🌍

SDG Youth Advocacy Training

🏅 Certificate Awarded

An intensive training program that equips young people with deep knowledge of all 17 SDGs and the advocacy skills needed to promote them in their communities.

  • Understanding the 2030 Agenda
  • Public speaking & advocacy skills
  • Community mobilization strategies
  • Certificate upon completion
👩‍💼

Youth Leadership Development

🏅 Certificate Awarded

Build the leadership, management, and communication skills needed to lead organizations, projects, and communities toward sustainable change.

  • Leadership styles & principles
  • Team building & conflict resolution
  • Project management fundamentals
  • Certificate upon completion
🌱

Climate Action & Environment

🏅 Certificate Awarded

Focused on SDGs 13, 14, and 15, this program trains youth to understand climate science and take practical environmental action in their communities.

  • Climate change science & impacts
  • Environmental advocacy campaigns
  • Sustainable living practices
  • Certificate upon completion
📊

Youth Entrepreneurship & Innovation

🏅 Certificate Awarded

Empowering youth to build sustainable businesses and social enterprises that create jobs and contribute to SDG 8 — Decent Work and Economic Growth.

  • Business idea development
  • Financial literacy & planning
  • Social enterprise models
  • Certificate upon completion
⚖️

Gender Equality & Inclusion

🏅 Certificate Awarded

Promoting SDG 5 and SDG 10, this program trains youth to champion gender equality, challenge discrimination, and build inclusive communities.

  • Gender rights & awareness
  • Campaigning for equality
  • Inclusive leadership practices
  • Certificate upon completion
🕊️

Peace, Justice & Governance

🏅 Certificate Awarded

Aligned with SDG 16, this program prepares youth to engage with governance systems, promote peace, and fight corruption and injustice in their communities.

  • Civic education & rights
  • Peacebuilding techniques
  • Anti-corruption advocacy
  • Certificate upon completion
